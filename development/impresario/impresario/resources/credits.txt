<h2>libavoid</h2>
<p>

and part of the <a href="http://www.adaptagrams.org/">adaptagrams project</a>.
It's source code is available on 
<a href="https://github.com/mjwybrow/adaptagrams/tree/master/cola/libavoid">GitHub</a>.
